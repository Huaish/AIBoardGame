遊戲規則

給定一 n 行 m 列的矩形棋盤，其中某些格子放置有黑色棋子，兩玩家輪流移動。 每次移動時，玩家可選擇至少有一顆棋子的縱向或橫向的任一行或列，將該行或列中的所有棋子全部取走。 當所有棋子都被取走時，遊戲結束，取走棋子數最多者獲勝。

操作方法

點擊棋盤上 1-9 或 A-Z 的格子，即可選擇該格子所在的行或列，並將該行或列中的所有棋子全部取走。 點擊 Take Back 按鈕，即可悔棋。 點擊 New Game 按鈕，即可開始新遊戲。 點擊 Attach txt file 按鈕，即可上傳初始盤面檔案檔案。

Game Rule

Given a n rows m columns rectangular board, some of the squares are placed with black pieces, and the two players take turns to move. Each time, the player can choose a vertical or horizontal row or column with at least one piece, and take away all the pieces in that row or column. When all the pieces are taken away, the game is over, and the one with the most pieces taken away wins.

How to play

Click on the squares 1-9 or A-Z on the board to select the row or column where the square is located, and take away all the pieces in that row or column. Click the Take Back button to undo the move. Click the New Game button to start a new game. Click the Attach txt file button to upload the initial board file.
